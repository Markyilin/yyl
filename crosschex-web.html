What’s CrossChex Standard?

CrossChex Standard solutions should top the list when you’re looking for time & attendance with access control. CrossChex Standard software for biometric access control and workforce management that allows capturing employees' clock-ins/outs and work activities for attendance tracking, compliance, and regional access control management. It is best for access restriction and attendance tracking of small and medium-sized businesses with a centralized workplace.

Whether it is an office, manufacturing plant, or any workplace setup that requires employees to punch in and out, our system delivers precise logging that is passive to the employee.

With managers worrying over how to maximize productivity, a natural step is tracking your employees' work hours and ensuring workplace security. The same badge can be used for employees to access the building and clock in and out. Additionally, the amount of hours spent by your HR team will be reduced with all the information in one place and reduced data entry time.
