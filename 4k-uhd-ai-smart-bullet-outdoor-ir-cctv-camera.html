Product Overview

iCam-B38Z is an outdoor bullet camera with 8MP/4K Ultra HD resolution and stylish design. The perfect combination of Intelligent Infrared and day/night function greatly improve the visibility effect in the dark and low-light environments. The 3X motorized lens can clearly auto adjust the images within 50 meters range. The IP66 design ensures you the flexbile installation in the outdoor environment. The iCam-B3 series supports standard H.264/H.265 and also the stanard onvif protocol. The optional WiFi Model(-W) or 4G Model(-G) offers wireless communication and easily setup. The edge storage SD card slot can support up to 128GB micro SD card. The device has built-in person detection and vehicle detection functions and you can easily get event alarm from the Intellisight mobile APP.
