Retail Security Solution

All retail businesses share similar operational priorities, which include reducing shoplifting, tracking customer foot traffic, managing user access, and monitoring properties remotely. Anviz is helping retail businesses simplify their security management workflow to increase productivity, security, and operations efficiency while reducing costs and optimizing processes for shared, single, and multiple locations for all types of retail environments.

    Detect and respond to threats as they occur with real-time alerts and 24/7 professional monitoring.

    Centralize physical security devices and empower users with an intuitive, easy-to-use platform.

  • Connect stores and align management

    Robust Architecture for Integration and Interoperability.

  • Optimize operations with data insights
