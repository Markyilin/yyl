Shipping

United States

We usually ship from our California fulfillment center within 1-2 business days. Orders being shipped within the continental USA to a home or business address (excludes PO/APO/FPO or other “non-physical” addresses) are delivered by UPS or USPS ground.

We provide tracking numbers for all orders. We will email you the tracking number once we deliver your order. Please track the status of your orders by clicking www.anviz.com/myorder.

Canada & Australia

We usually ship via our Amazon FBA within 1-2 business days. Amazon functions as our logistics partner to ship our products. They will choose the carrier that they think works best for our customers.

We provide tracking numbers for all orders. We will email you the tracking number within 2-6 business days after we deliver your order.
