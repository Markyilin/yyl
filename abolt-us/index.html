Sustainability at Anviz

Environmental, Social and Corporate Governance.

  • We address global environmental challenges

    Anviz aims to deliver smart touchless access control and time attendance technology to minimize any negative impact that plastic cards, mechanical keys and traditional disks may have on the environment. Wherever possible, we design and engineer our product packaging with “minimizing environmental impact” as an integral part of our design brief. Our raw material sourcing is carefully curated to reduce our carbon footprint.

    Our global manufacturing base is nearly powered by 100% clean and renewable energy. Part of that energy comes from our own on-site solar panels.
