What is W Series?

W Series is a fully new and powerful series launched by Anviz team for the modern and cloud time & attendance and standalone access control management. W Series uses a 1Ghz fast CPU and is  Linux which ensuresmparison time of less 0.5 seconds under 1: 1000 status. A new larger fingerprint sensor is used to increase the accuracy and adapt to all day time environment standard EM card reader and WiFi function is also equipped for W Series to realize flexible and portable applications.
